2.Events Rules and Regulations

RULES AND REGULATIONS

FUN ZONE
  RANGOLI
  1. Only one entry per institution is allowed.
  2. One person one rangoli
  3. Bring your own materials
  4. Spot entry will not be entertained
  5. The judge's decision will be final
   PAINTING
  1. One person one painting
  2. Theme (your own interested area).
  3. Bring your own materials
  4. Speak on the painting for 1 or 2 minutes
  5. Spot entry will not be entertained
  6. The judge's decision will be final
   COOKING WITHOUT FIRE
  1. It is a team event or an individual event
  2. Each team comprises of 1 to 4 members
  3. One team one item
  4. Bring your own materials
  5. Spot entry will not be entertained
  6. The judge's decision will be final.
   TREASURE HUNT
  1. It is a team event & comprises of 4 to 6 member.
  2. Spot entry will not be entertained
  3. The judge's decision will be final.
   PHOTOGRAPHY
  1. One person one photo is allowed
  2. Theme (Nature or Historical Places).
  3. Spot entry will not be entertained
  4. The judge's decision will be final
   ETHNIC SHOW
  1. It's an individual even
  2. Strictly maintain the timing
  3. Speak on the costumes for 1 or 2 minutes
  4. Spot entry will not be entertained
  5. The judge's decision will be final

TECHNICAL EVENT
  GENERAL OR TECHNICAL DEBATE
  1. One person from one department
  2. Spot entry will not be entertained
  3. The judge's decision will be final
   TECHNICAL/GENERAL QUIZ
  1. Quiz will be conducted for Mechanical and Civil branches separately.
  2. Quiz will be conducted for Circuit branches separately
  3. There will be total five group and each group having Four students.
  4. There will be five rounds and each round will have ten questions. Each group will get two questions for every round.
  5. Group will get ten marks for each correct answer , no negative marking for wrong answer.
  6. Each question will get 60 seconds
  7. if the group cannot answer the question, then the question would be forwarded to next group and they get five marks.
  8. If there is the TIE between two teams then there will be tiebreaker question.
  9. Spot entry will not be entertained
  10. The judge's decision will be final
   PAPER/POSTER PRESENTATION
  1. 15 slides are allowed for paper presentation
  2. Time Provided for each Participant is 10 minutes
  3. The judges decision will be final
  4. Spot entry will not be entertained
  5. The judge's decision will be final
   CLAY MODELLING
  1. Limited Space will be provided
  2. Spot entry will not be entertained
  3. The judge's decision will be final
  CIRCUIT ANALYSIS
  1. One person one circuit
  2. Explanation of circuit.
  3. Spot entry will not be entertained
  4. The judge's decision will be final
   ROBOTICS
  1. C, Python , Java languages for coding
  2. Spot entry will not be entertained
  3. The judge's decision will be final
   3D MODELLING
  1. Limited Space will be provided
  2. Spot entry will not be entertained
  3. The judge's decision will be final
  BEST MANAGER & SHARP THINK
  1. One person from each department
  2. Spot entry will not be entertained
  3. The judge's decision will be final
